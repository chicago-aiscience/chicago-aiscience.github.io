# Clinical Knowledge Graph Open Source LLM

> Authors: Shira Lupkin, Rui Ding, Didem Çifçi

{button}`GitHub Repository <https://github.com/chicago-aiscience/Clinical_KG_OS_LLM>`

*AI Schmidt Hackathon 2026. Participants are tasked with building a multi-agent Knowledge Graph extraction pipeline that transforms unstructured clinical transcripts into structured patient representations — capturing diagnoses, symptoms, treatments, and their relationships — to enable faster clinical handoffs, smarter decision support, and better patient care.*
